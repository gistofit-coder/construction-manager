💾 Automatic Database Backup

Creates a ZIP of: database + all receipt PDFs + all cert PDFs. Runs automatically on shutdown. Configure folder and retention in Company Settings.

Past backups:
✅ Verify Receipts

Scans all ledger entries with receipt filenames and verifies they exist on disk. Updates the ✅/⚠️ indicator in the Ledger.

📂 Scan COI PDFs

Scans the Certs Folder for PDFs named CompanyName MM-DD-YY_MM-DD-YY.pdf. Matches each PDF to a contractor, reads dates from the filename, and creates or updates COI records. Also verifies all existing cert records against files on disk. Duplicate COIs (same contractor + same expiry date) are skipped automatically.
